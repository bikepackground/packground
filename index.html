GAIAGPS ROUTES LEGACY

For those planning their own trips, the route descriptions found below remain as our legacy of a wild time out there.

Altay Quest: Borderland of Mongolia and Russia

(Central Asia | Remote Wilderness)
∨

A demanding journey crossing two national parks and a UNESCO site. Linking the Mongolian and Russian Altay via ancient nomad paths and the mystical Ukok Plateau. Expect steep gradients and trailless hike-a-bike passes.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

Bike Baikal: Winter Bikepacking in Siberia

(Russia | Winter Expedition)
∨

An expedition across the world’s second-largest freshwater lake in brutal Siberian winter. Ride over meter-thick black ice, navigate pressure ridges, and endure temperatures down to -30°C.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

High Atlas Divide: Morocco Challenge

(Africa | Mountain Culture)
∨

Traveling back in time through the ancestral home of the Berbers. This connoisseur’s route crosses the range four times and conquers ten major passes via ancient mule tracks and rugged dirt paths.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

Illampu Circuit: Bikepacking Version

(Bolivia | High Altitude)
∨

One of the most spectacular glacier tracks in the Andes. A wild loop circumnavigating the mighty Illampu (6,368m), featuring high-altitude passes over 5,000 meters in the Cordillera Real.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

Desert Spoor: Jordan and Israel

(Middle East | Desert Singletrack)
∨

A journey through rusty red dunes and deep-cut wadis. Features 300 kilometers of world-class, purpose-built singletrack on the Israel Bike Trail through the profound silence of the Negev.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

Nomad Trails: Mongolia

(Central Asia | Infinite Horizons)
∨

A bikepacking paradise of vast steppe and rugged mountains. Experience a country where nomadic culture is life, navigating an infinite network of dirt tracks through magical light and angry winds.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

Pioneer Trail: USA Wild West Wonders

(USA | Desert Landscapes)
∨

Discover Utah’s slot canyons and rock formations off the beaten path. Traverses the Grand Staircase-Escalante and Capitol Reef, concluding in the hidden gem of Lockhart Basin near Moab.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

Puna Loop: Northwest Argentina

(South America | High Mountain Desert)
∨

A gargantuan lunar landscape of stone, sand, and salt lakes. Experience the raw beauty of the Andes Puna, where volcanic cones cast long shadows and the solitude is as deep as the silence.

VIEW ON GAIAGPS

The Yak Track: Eastern Tibet

(Asia | High Plateau Culture)
∨

Traversing the historic provinces of Kham and Amdo. Over twenty passes exceeding 4,000m, connecting remote monasteries and Khampa nomad camps via ancient hiking trails and dirt roads.
